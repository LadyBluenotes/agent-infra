## Review Cadence

Suggested cadence: monthly or after every 10 logged prompts (whichever comes first).

Review checklist:
- Identify repeated prompt patterns with inconsistent skill choices.
- Add or refine prompt-to-skill mappings in `@skills/general/skill-retrieval.md` if needed.
- Note any routing mistakes and the corrected skill path.
