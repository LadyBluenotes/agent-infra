# Skill: SEO Audit

## Apply When
- Auditing or diagnosing SEO issues.

## Do
- Confirm scope (site-wide vs pages) and goals (traffic, conversions, specific keywords).
- Check crawl/index basics (robots.txt, sitemap, canonical, noindex).
- Check on-page basics (title/H1, meta description, internal links, thin/duplicate pages).
- Check performance signals (Core Web Vitals / mobile).
- Produce a prioritized action list with evidence.

## Don't
- Don't claim ranking outcomes; stick to fixes and likely impact.

## Output
- Executive summary + prioritized issues (impact, evidence, fix).
