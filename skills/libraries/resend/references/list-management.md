# List Management

## Suppression Rules

- Hard bounces: suppress immediately
- Complaints: suppress immediately
- Soft bounces: suppress after 3+ failures

## Hygiene Tasks

- Remove invalid addresses
- Re-engage inactive users before removal
- Monitor bounce and complaint rates weekly

## Metrics Targets

- Bounce rate < 2%
- Complaint rate < 0.05%

## Retention Guidance

- Send logs: 90 days
- Delivery logs: 90 days
- Bounce/complaint history: 3 years
- Suppression list: indefinite
- Consent records: 3 years after expiry

## Separation

- Keep transactional and marketing lists separate
- Apply suppressions to both
- Unsubscribe applies to marketing only
